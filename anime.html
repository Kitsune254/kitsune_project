Anime

One of my favourite pass time is watching anime.
Now many people would say that anime is for children or they are just meaningless cartoons. But what i like about anime is the creativeness and the imaginative power of the creators.

Apart from the creative prowess of the writers i also like some of the values that animes present to society.

Naruto

One of my favourite anime is Naruto.
It mainly focuses on the importance of teamwork and how important friends are in this society of ours. It is about ninjas who go through a war just because of the hatred the is in the world and how some people do evil deeds without caring of the effect on other people

Blue Lock

This is another anime that caught my eye.
Because of my love for football this anime really interested me because it is about finding the greatest striker in Japan.
It comes after Japan's disappointing effort in the 2018 world cup tournament

Jujutsu Kaisen

This anime doesn't have anything special about it.
I just watched it because it had perfect graphics and characters. It is just about curses that roam the streets of Tokyo and specially trained sorcerers that exorcise the curses.
